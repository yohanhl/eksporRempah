Red Ginger

Red Ginger (Zingiber officinale var. rubrum) is a type of rhizome plant that is well-known for its use as a medicinal plant and spices. In Indonesia, red ginger is a type that is widely planted by farmers, especially in Sumatra and Java. Because it contains the oleoresin, gingerol, and zingeron substances, it also has the most essential oils compared to other types of ginger. Red ginger in our land is grown and cared for using organic fertilizers and free from pests and fungicides.

Red Ginger

Red Ginger (Zingiber officinale var. rubrum) is a type of rhizome plant that is well-known for its use as a medicinal plant and spices. In Indonesia, red ginger is a type that is widely grown by farmers, especially in Sumatra and Java. Because it contains the oleoresin, gingerol, and zingeron, ginger also has the most essential oils compared to other types of ginger. The red ginger in our soil is grown and cared for using organic fertilizers and is free of pests and fungicides. The ginger we harvest is whole large ginger, free from soil and foreign matter. We sliced/split then dried in the sun.

  • Origin : Java, Sumatera
  • Moisture : 40-45%
