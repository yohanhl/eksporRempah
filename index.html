Facts about Indonesia

With its rich in natural resources, making Indonesia called "Heaven on Earth" and also because of its beauty and biodiversity. Indonesia has fertile soil that makes the country is one of the most well-known agricultural countries that produce high-quality plant-based commodities, especially spices, as it is one of the leading commodities in Indonesia.

Indonesia has many volcanoes that can fertilize the soil. Volcanic eruptions release volcanic ash, which contains good minerals and is very useful for plant growth. Therefore, various types of plants can grow well. In addition, the soil with lots of nutrients and a tropical climate with sufficient sunlight and rainfall are the advantages of Indonesia. Indonesian land can be planted with various types of plants such as spices and provide superior results so that it is feasible to compete in the international market.
