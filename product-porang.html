Konjac Chips

Konjac (Amorphophallus konjac) is a type of tuber plant that grows in subtropical to tropical areas. In Indonesia, konjac is called porang/iles-iles. Konjac contains high levels of glucomannan, which is why it is good to use it as diet food. Konjac tubers are processed by slicing into chips, then making flour. Konjac flour is a raw material for making glue, cosmetics, and shirataki. We provide konjac in the form of chips with a sun-drying process and oven.

Specifications :

  • Origin : Java
  • Moisture : 13-15%
  • Impurities : Max 3%
  • Thickneess : 3-5mm
  • Form : Cleaned Dried Chips
